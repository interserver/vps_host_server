IMPORTANT — Activate your license:
  1. Find your Installation Token ID in /root/swpanel-credentials.txt
     (panel install runs in the background for several minutes after boot).
  2. Go to https://go.swpanel.com/activate/
  3. Choose "Manual installation" / "Ya tengo mi Token ID".
  4. Enter the Token ID and click "Verificar Token ID".
  5. Select your license and click "Activar SWPanel ahora".
