Octelium (zero-trust secure access platform) is installed and ready.

The Octelium cluster authenticates with an auth token, not a web password.
The token is generated at first boot and saved on the server.

SSH access:
  ssh root@{$ip}
  Password: {$password}

Next steps:
  1. SSH in and read your cluster auth token:
       cat /root/octelium-credentials.txt
  2. Create the required DNS records (cluster will not work without them):
       A     <your-domain>    -> {$ip}
       CNAME *.<your-domain>  -> <your-domain>
  3. From your local machine:
       curl -fsSL https://octelium.com/install.sh | bash
       octelium login --domain <your-domain> --auth-token <TOKEN>
  4. WireGuard gateway: UDP 53820. LE/admin email used: {$email}.
  5. Full details are in /root/octelium-credentials.txt
