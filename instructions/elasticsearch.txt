Elasticsearch is ready.

Endpoint: http://127.0.0.1:9200  (bound to localhost only — not exposed publicly)

Built-in superuser login:
  Username: elastic
  Password: {$password}

Server (SSH):
  ssh root@{$ip}
  Root password: {$password}

Access from your workstation (SSH tunnel):
  ssh -L 9200:127.0.0.1:9200 root@{$ip}
  then open http://localhost:9200 in your browser.

Quick health check (run on the server):
  curl -u elastic:'{$password}' http://127.0.0.1:9200/_cluster/health?pretty

Next steps:
  1. Use the SSH tunnel above, or set network.host in
     /etc/elasticsearch/elasticsearch.yml to expose it (secure it first).
  2. Full credentials are in /root/elasticsearch-credentials.txt on the server.
