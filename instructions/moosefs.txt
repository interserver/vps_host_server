MooseFS is ready.

The CGI web interface listens on localhost only (port 9425) for security.
Reach it through an SSH tunnel:
  ssh -L 9425:127.0.0.1:9425 root@{$ip}
  (password: {$password})
Then open in your browser:
  http://127.0.0.1:9425/mfs.cgi

Admin password: {$password}
Admin / Let's Encrypt email: {$email}

SSH access:
  ssh root@{$ip}
  (password: {$password})

Mount the filesystem from a client (needs moosefs-client installed):
  mfsmount /mnt/mfs -H {$ip}
