hosted on this server; you manage it from ServerWand's website.

Next steps:
  1. Log in to https://manage.serverwand.com
  2. Create -> Server -> Custom / Other provider, and enter:
       Host: {$ip}   User: root   Password: {$password}   Port: 22
  3. ServerWand connects over SSH and installs Apache, MariaDB, PHP, Postfix,
     etc., then manages sites, databases, SSL, and backups remotely.
  4. After ServerWand deploys its key you may disable password SSH and rotate
     the root password. Details are in /root/serverwand-credentials.txt.
